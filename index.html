About Us

Think Ahead of Customer

Taizhou Cypress Import & Export Co., Ltd is a professional sport shoes trading company based in Taizhou, Zhejiang Province, China — the heartland of China's footwear manufacturing industry.

We specialize in sourcing, developing, and exporting kids, men's, and women's sport shoes to global buyers. Our strength lies in our deep factory network, experienced merchandising team, and a relentless focus on quality and on-time delivery.

Whether you're a brand looking for OEM manufacturing, a distributor seeking consistent supply, or a retailer building your own line — we're here to make your sourcing simple and successful.
